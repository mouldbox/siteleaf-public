Supplier Standards

Plyable delivers the world’s best designed and manufactured composites moulds, making consistent and reliable quality the foundation of the Plyable brand. To achieve this, we demand industry-leading standards from all of our manufacturing partners. Our suppliers have gone through the Plyable verification process, which ensures our network delivers the high quality standards, responsiveness and data security protection that our customers expect from Plyable.

Our supplier network contains massive manufacturing capacity and wide-ranging capabilities allowing us to identify the best supplier bespoke for your order, no matter the engineering challenge, material, tolerance, size or lead time.

Plyable’s partners have access to our technologies and a back office portal, enabling the advantages of the Plyable technology to filter into our supply chain. If you would like to become part of the Plyable supplier network please email info@Plyable.co.uk.

Confidentiality

Plyable anonymises all client identifying data such as company names on drawings and part numbers before it is shared with any of our suppliers. This protects our customers and ourselves. See our security guide and privacy policy for more information.
